01 At a glance

Tribespot is a web-based recruitment software. Established in 2019, the software was built by student society executives who have run some of the biggest student organisations in Australia. Having experienced the pain of annual leadership recruitment, Tribespot streamline the recruitment process.

Design Process

02 The Problem

Every society at UNSW has at least registered on Tribespot and have made a recruitment drive. However, after making the drive, not all societies have stayed on, with some societies going back to use google forms. Potential pain-points that users may experience might involve

  • Outdated visual design: The application does not appeal to people on first impression.
  • Hard-to-use and non-intuitive user interface: Some features & functionalities may be quite hard to understand / different from what they are used to.

How might Tribespot improve its retention rate from societies whilst ensuring that all new societies are also onboarded with Tribespot?
